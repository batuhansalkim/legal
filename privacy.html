Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

AI Visa Guide ("we", "us") is operated by Eralab Software. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data We Collect

  • Passport country: Stored locally on your device only. Never sent to our servers.
  • AI queries: Your travel questions are sent to Google Gemini API to generate responses. We do not store these queries.
  • Purchase info: Managed by Apple and RevenueCat. We do not see your payment details.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email, location, or any personally identifiable information. All app preferences are stored locally on your device via AsyncStorage.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store – handles payments. See Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
